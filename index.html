About Us

About VINOTHAM

Vinotham Architects (VA) is a professionally managed architecture and design firm having its offices in Avudaiyarkoil, Pudukkottai and Chennai. The firm had its inception in 2010 under Architect N. Vinothkumar, who was the pioneer in creating awareness of architecture as a profession in Avudaiyarkoil, India. With a huge clientele, rich experience in the field and exploration into various typologies, VA has its growth laid on strong foundation

We are evolving in architecture step by step and started our carier as a freelancer in pudukkottai, trichy and chennai after we started our design studio named Abscissa Design Studio later we developed our Studio as an Architecture Firm in 2018.

Philosophy

Architecture imparts emotions which differ from person to person based on their perspective, The philosophy of Vinotham is to concisely build the Architecture so as to perceive Positive Emotions.
